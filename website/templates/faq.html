Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the LeeBRA Chat Application! If you have any questions about using our application, please see the Frequently Asked Questions below.

When loading our application, you will land on the Log In page.

To create a new account, navigate to the Sign Up button.

Once you are on the Sign Up form, you will be prompted to create a username, enter your preferred display name (this is what other users will see you as), and enter/confirm your password.

The default landing page for the LeeBRA chat application is the login page.

To log in to your account, enter your username and password into the respective fields and click the Log In button.

You're in luck! If you have forgotten your password, simply click the Forgot Password button on the Log In page and follow the prompts to recover or change your password.

To start chatting with someone, you must first friend them.

To friend someone, first search for their username using the Search page, then click the 'Add' button next to it. You may message them, but they must also add you as a friend to view your messages!

If you are unable to chat with someone, they may have blocked you.

There is no way to contact someone who has blocked you through the website..

Simply click on their profile and select the Block button located in the top-right of the screen.

You can unblock someone by searching for their name, clicking on their profile, and clicking Unblock, or through your Block List located under your profile.

In a chat with a friend or a group chat, you may click the X button to the left of your message to delete it.

In the Settings page, there are options to change text size, text color, and message background color!
