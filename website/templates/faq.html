Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the LeeBRA Chat Application! If you have any questions about using our application, please see the Frequently Asked Questions below.

When loading our application, you will land on the Log In page.

To create a new account, navigate to the Sign Up button.

Once you are on the Sign Up form, you will be prompted to create a username, enter your preferred display name (this is what other users will see you as), and enter/confirm your password.

The default landing page for the LeeBRA chat application is the login page.

To log in to your account, enter your username and password into the respective fields and click the Log In button.

You're in luck! If you have forgotten your password, simply click the Forgot Password button on the Log In page and follow the prompts to recover or change your password.
